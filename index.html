Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Do the three brands operate independently or together?

Both. While you can hire D.R.O.P Kitchen purely for a private dinner or MontyB purely for a portrait, they are designed to integrate. For example, a restaurant client might use The Student & The Teacher for brand strategy, D.R.O.P Kitchen for menu consulting, and MontyB for food photography. It is one unified architecture.

Are the digital products suitable for absolute beginners?

Yes. The Student & The Teacher designs systems (like the ENFJ Prompt Pack or Notion templates) specifically to reduce the learning curve. They bridge the gap between complex behavioral science and practical, everyday application.

Does MontyB Photography travel outside of Minneapolis?

Yes. While based in the Twin Cities, MontyB is available for national and international travel for editorial, lifestyle, and documentary assignments. Travel fees are factored into custom quotes.

How do I book a private chef experience?

Start by submitting the Client Evaluation form above, selecting "D.R.O.P Kitchen" as your primary interest. Detail your desired date, guest count, and any known dietary preferences. We will respond with availability and a baseline menu proposal.
