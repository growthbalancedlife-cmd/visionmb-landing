FAQ

Questions We Hear Often

What exactly is the Vision:MB Ecosystem?
+

Vision:MB is a multi-brand creative ecosystem based in Minneapolis, MN. It houses three distinct brands — The Student & The Teacher (education and strategy), D.R.O.P Kitchen (culinary arts and private chef), and MontyB Photography (visual storytelling) — all operating under one unified philosophy: mastery, intentionality, and legacy-driven work. Each brand feeds the others, creating a full-spectrum creative and business service ecosystem.

How do the three brands connect to each other?
+

By design. A D.R.O.P Kitchen dining event gets documented by MontyB Photography. The resulting content becomes brand IP, packaged into educational products through The Student & The Teacher. The cycle continues — content becomes IP, IP becomes products, products build community. Every brand is a spoke in the same wheel, turning together.

Do you work with clients outside of Minneapolis?
+

Absolutely. Digital products and strategic systems consulting are 100% remote digital models and available globally. Photography deployments are centered in Minneapolis but available for strategic travel. Private culinary bookings are regional focus with options for destination projects.
