Vision:MB — Brand Overview

What Vision:MB Is

Vision:MB isn’t a single business — it’s an operating philosophy made visible across disciplines. Founded by Monty B, a Minneapolis-based photographer, private chef, creative director, and healing-focused educator, Vision:MB brings three distinct crafts under one coherent identity.

The name represents exactly what it is: a vision — deliberate, expansive, and personal. Every arm of the ecosystem operates independently but draws from the same root: the belief that human beings are whole, that creativity is healing, and that excellence in any craft requires the same attention, depth, and intention.

Whether you’re hiring MontyB Photography for a brand shoot, booking D.R.O.P Kitchen for a private dining experience, or working through the Traumatic Intelligence series, you’re engaging the same philosophy — applied differently.

The 360° Philosophy

Why Three Businesses— and Why They’re One

Most people compartmentalize creativity. Vision:MB doesn’t. The 360° holistic model at the center of this ecosystem is the conviction that growth — personal, professional, creative — happens in the whole body, not just the mind.

A photographer who understands food styling creates more evocative images. A private chef who understands healing creates more intentional tables. A healing practitioner who understands systems and design creates more effective frameworks. These aren’t separate skill sets — they are one integrated practice at different angles.

Vision:MB exists for clients, students, and collaborators who see this too — who want to work with someone who brings the whole picture, not just one piece of it.

“” — Monty B, Vision:MB
